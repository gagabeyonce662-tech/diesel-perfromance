About Us

The GTA's Trusted Diesel Specialists

Diesel Performance has served truck owners, operators and fleets across the GTA and surrounding areas with expert truck repair, maintenance and diagnostic services for over a decade. Founded by Harsh Verma, we've built a reputation on honesty, precision and respect for your time.

From routine oil services to complex engine inframes and DEF/SCR systems, we specialise in Volvo and Mack trucks — both new and old — and our quick turnaround time has made us the go-to shop for drivers across Mississauga.

What We Do

Our Services

Comprehensive commercial truck repair and maintenance — everything your fleet needs under one roof.

Brake System Repair

Complete brake inspections, repairs and line replacements. We keep your rig stopping safely and on spec with CVIP standards.

Oil Services

Engine oil changes, filter replacement and fluid inspections using commercial-grade lubricants matched to your engine specs.

Computer Diagnostics

Advanced OBD and manufacturer-level diagnostics for Volvo, Mack and all major commercial brands. Find the fault fast.

Transmission Repair & Rebuild

From minor adjustments to full rebuilds on manual and automatic transmissions — we do it all in-house.

Engine Inframes

In-frame overhauls and complete engine rebuilds performed by experienced technicians with precision and care.

HVAC / Heating & AC

Cabin heating and air conditioning diagnostics, repair and recharge so your driver stays comfortable year-round.

5th Wheel Service

5th wheel inspection, lubrication, adjustment and replacement for safe trailer coupling on every run.

Routine Maintenance

Scheduled maintenance programs designed to keep your fleet running efficiently and reduce costly downtime.

DEF / SCR Systems

Diesel Exhaust Fluid system diagnostics, DEF pump replacement and SCR catalyst repair for emissions compliance.
